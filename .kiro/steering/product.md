# Product Overview

Tabashir is a comprehensive HR consulting and job search platform consisting of two applications:

## Web Application (Next.js)
A full-featured HR platform serving candidates, recruiters, and administrators. Key capabilities:
- Job posting and management for recruiters
- AI-powered resume optimization using OpenAI GPT-4o
- Job matching and application tracking for candidates
- Payment processing via Stripe for premium services (AI job apply, LinkedIn optimization, interview training)
- Admin dashboard with role-based permissions
- Multilingual support (English/Arabic with RTL)

## Mobile Application (Flutter)
A cross-platform mobile app for job seekers with:
- Job search and application management
- AI-powered resume builder
- Profile and notification management
- Subscription services with Stripe integration
- Offline-first architecture with local database

## User Types
- **Candidates**: Job seekers using the platform to find jobs, optimize resumes, and apply
- **Recruiters**: Companies posting jobs and managing applications
- **Admins**: Platform administrators with granular permission system (Super Admin and Regular Admin roles)

## Core Services
- AI Job Apply (200 AED): Automated job applications to 200 matched positions
- LinkedIn Optimization (60 AED): Profile enhancement service
- ATS CV Optimization (40 AED): Resume formatting for applicant tracking systems
- Interview Training (150 AED): Interview preparation service
