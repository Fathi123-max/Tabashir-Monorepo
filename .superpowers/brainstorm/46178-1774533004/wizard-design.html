Onboarding Wizard Design

A clear, non-AI-blocking flow that captures requirements and then processes them all at once.

Step-by-Step Flow

Wizard Steps (5 Total)
1
Resume Upload: User selects PDF/DOCX. No AI parsing yet (fast).
2
Target Roles: Multi-select from common roles or manual entry.
3
Locations: Select preferred cities/countries.
4
Personal Details: Confirm Nationality and Gender.
5
Final Review & Processing: User clicks "Start My Career Journey" → add_client runs with full AI matching.

Key Components

  • OnboardingCubit: Manages the wizard's local state (file bytes, selected roles, locations, details).
  • OnboardingScreen: A multi-page view using PageView to transition smoothly between steps.
  • ProcessingView: A beautiful, animated loading state shown while the AI is matching jobs.

User Experience

  • Fast initial upload — no waiting at the start.
  • Clear expectations — the "Processing" step at the end feels substantial.
  • Highly structured data — ensures the AI has exactly what it needs for the best match.
