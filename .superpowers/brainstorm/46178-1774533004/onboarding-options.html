How should we handle onboarding?

Pick the approach that best fits the desired user experience.

A

Multi-Step Wizard

After registration, the user imports their resume, then sees a clear 3-step wizard: 1. Roles, 2. Locations, 3. Personal Details. Finally, it triggers add_client and goes home.

B

Quick Entry Screen

A single, unified screen after resume import that captures all requirements at once. Best for power users who want to get straight to matching.

C

Smart Extraction + Confirmation

We use the AI-parsed resume data to pre-fill the requirements. The user just reviews and clicks "Confirm & Apply" to trigger add_client.

Why do this?

  • Higher quality AI matching results.
  • Users get immediate value right after signing up.
  • Reduces friction in finding the first job.

Challenges

  • Adds 1-2 minutes to the signup process.
  • Requires careful UI design to avoid overwhelming the user.
