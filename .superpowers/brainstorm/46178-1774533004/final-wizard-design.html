Final Onboarding Wizard Design

A tailored UAE-first onboarding experience for new candidates.

The Complete Onboarding Flow

Onboarding Wizard (5 Steps)
1
Resume Upload: User selects PDF/DOCX.
Backend Suggests Titles in the background.
2
Target Roles: Multi-select chips pre-filled by /suggest-job-titles.
3
UAE Locations: Interactive grid of UAE Cities:
Dubai Abu Dhabi Sharjah Ajman
4
Personal Details: Nationality and Gender confirmation.
5
Processing: Elegant loading screen → add_client call → Home Dashboard.

Technical Architecture

  • Navigation: Triggered immediately after Signup success. Replaces resumeImport route with onboardingWizard.
  • API Integration: Adds suggestJobTitles and addClient to TabashirApiService.
  • State: OnboardingCubit handles cross-step data persistence and final submission logic.
